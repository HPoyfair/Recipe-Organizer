Caesar Salad
Ingredients:

Lettuce
Croutons
Parmesan
Caesar dressing
Instructions:

Rinse and chop the lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Place the lettuce in a large salad bowl.
Pour the Caesar dressing over the lettuce and toss until the leaves are evenly coated.
Add croutons to the salad, mixing gently to combine.
Sprinkle freshly grated Parmesan cheese on top.
Serve immediately to maintain the crispness of the lettuce and croutons.
