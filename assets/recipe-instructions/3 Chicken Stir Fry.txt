Cut the chicken breast into thin strips.
Heat oil in a wok or large frying pan over high heat.
Add minced garlic and ginger, stir-fry until fragrant.
Add the chicken strips and cook until browned.
Toss in your choice of vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, broccoli, carrots).
Add soy sauce and stir-fry for another 5 minutes.
Serve hot over rice or noodles.
